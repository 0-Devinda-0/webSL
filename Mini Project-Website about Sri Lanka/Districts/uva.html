The Nine Arches Bridge

The Nine Arches Bridge also known as The Bridge In the Sky is one of the best examples of colonial-era railway construction in the country. It is located in Demodara, between Ella and Demodara railway stations.

Yala National Park

Yala National Park is the second largest national park in Sir Lanka and also the most visited national park. It covers around 980 square kilometers. The park is best known for its variety of wild animals. It is important for the conservation of Sri Lankan elephants, Sri Lankan leopards and aquatic birds.
