Elephant Orphanage

Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage is an orphanage, nursery and captive breeding ground for wild Asian elephants.Pinnawala has the largest herd of captive elephants in the world.

Adam's Peak

Adam's Peak, also known as Sri Pada is a conical mountain in Sri Lanka. It is well known place to see the sunrise hence it is one of the great views you can get of a sunrise.
